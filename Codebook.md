subject.id           

      Integer identifying each of the 30 subjects
      
activity                        
      
      Activity in which the variables were measured. Can adopt six values:
            1. WALKING
            2. WALKING_UPSTAIRS
            3. WALKING_DOWNSTAIRS
            4. SITTING
            5. STANDING
            6. LAYING
